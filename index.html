Welcome

Part of an art installation with the Harvestworks International Artists' Collective. This code retrieves #harvestworks tagged tweets from Twitter and sends them to a screen, and to another (not existing here) max patch for audio processing.

Artists and Contributors

Original work included algorythmic composition by Joshua Booth, sculpture by Minah Park, art direction by Daniel Belquer.
